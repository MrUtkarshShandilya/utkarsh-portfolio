GOLD·MUTH

Gold Futures & Muthoot Finance — A Time Series Study

Advanced Business Statistics Portfolio · Stata

Built entirely from a standing start in Stata: cleaned and merged raw price data, log-transformed both series, read ACF/PACF correlograms, ran ADF unit root tests, tested for Engle-Granger cointegration, then modelled the short-run dynamics with an Error Correction Model. Both Gold Futures and Muthoot Finance turned out to be I(1) — non-stationary in levels — with no formal long-run cointegration at conventional significance, but a statistically significant short-run relationship captured by the ECM.
